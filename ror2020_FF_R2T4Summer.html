Why is this survey required?

The survey is required if you have dropped courses, withdrawn, received a negative grade, or have a missing grade from a current session. Per federal regulations, we must obtain survey or email confirmation of your intent to participate in future sessions. We are not able to assume intent to participate based solely on enrollment in a future session. If this survey confirmation is not received within the 10 day timeframe we must treat you as withdrawn and reevaluate aid eligibility based on the withdraw dates or participation reports that are recorded for earlier sessions.

FERPA is the Family Education Rights and Privacy Act, the law that limits the release of student information. Students, you have the right to your financial aid records (the status of your file, your financial aid award, information on the FAFSA, etc.) and to decide who else may have access to your financial aid information. Thus requests for or disclosure of financial aid information cannot be released to parent(s), other individuals or agencies without your written consent.
